What I Do

Yaw Andoh Mensah is a passionate young man called to the do the Work of the Apostle and live the Life of the Prophet. He was called into ministry in 2012 and has been the Chief Steward pushing the Ingenious Agenda of the Kingdom of God.

He is an anointed and dynamic teacher of the Infallible Word of God. He is a Conference Speaker and a Prolific Writer. He is also married with three children and lives in Ghana, West Africa.

Yaw Andoh is the founder and President of the Yaw Andoh Mensah Ministries and the Ingenious Network. His desire is to see Jesus return to the earth and reign as the Scripture reveals.

In order to see his dream come true, he has given himself to the Studying of Scriptures, Prayer and Teaching to prepare the body of Christ for the Second Coming of the Lord Jesus Christ and the age to come.

Events

Contention Camp is a Youth Camp organized bi-annually to help young people grow in their faith and commitment to excellence in the world. We train participants through career counselling, soft skills training, apologetics, habit formation and Bible studies

Ingenious TV is an online Christian Content producer. Our desire is to see the transformation Jesus promised through the Holy Spirit practically experienced by the people of the earth.

Ingenious Jesus Conference is an annual conference that is organized to invigorate and stirr up the passion, purpose and drive of believers to go into the world and achieve great things for the Lord.
